The Daily Timeline

Strategic administration is crucial. This timeline dictates exactly when each compound should be introduced to maximize bioavailability and prevent negative interactions.

Morning: Activation

Focus on dopamine support & foundational nutrients.

  • The Core Core: Elvanse 30mg + L-Theanine 200mg (Stacked together)
  • Pre/With Core: L-Tyrosine 1g
  • With Food (Fat): Omega-3 (2 caps), D3 (62.5µg), K2 (100µg)
  • With Any Food: Creatine (3g), L-Arginine (3g)
  • General AM: Ginkgo Biloba 120mg, Mag Citrate 125mg, Cocolytes (Water)

Afternoon: Maintenance

Sustaining levels & hard cutoffs.

  • Empty Stomach (>2h post-Elvanse): NAC 700mg
  • As Needed (Pre-15:00): Dexfarm 5mg

Evening: Downregulation

Focus on nervous system relaxation & sleep prep.

  • Early Evening: Vit C 500mg + Zinc 4mg (Away from Elvanse/Mg)
  • Post-18:00: Valerian Tincture (30 drops)
  • Pre-Sleep (30-60m): Melatonin 2mg, Tryptophan 220mg, Mag Citrate 125mg
  • SOS Only: Zopiclone 7.5mg (Flag if >3x/week)

Critical Interaction Matrix

✓ Synergies & Co-factors

  • Elvanse + L-Theanine: Theanine smooths out the stimulant curve.
  • D3 + K2 + Fatty Food: Vitamins are fat-soluble; K2 directs D3-absorbed calcium into bones.
  • Tyrosine -> Elvanse: Provides raw dopamine precursors for the medication to utilize.

⚠ Conflicts & Separations

  • Vit C vs Elvanse: Vitamin C (ascorbic acid) increases excretion rates of amphetamines. Take PM only.
  • Vit C vs Magnesium: Separating prevents potential minor absorption competition.
  • NAC vs Elvanse: NAC can blunt stimulant effects. Maintain strict 2+ hour gap.

⏰ Temporal Strictures

  • L-Tyrosine: Do not take after 17:00 (insomnia risk).
  • Dexfarm: Hard cutoff at 15:00 (half-life sleep disruption).
  • Zopiclone: Absolute limit: 3x/week max to prevent tolerance/dependence.
